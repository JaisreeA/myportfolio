It has limited flexibility and needed more safety concerns.Inorder to overcome this problem ,we came up with an idea of Charge Pump. It works on the principle of voltage doubler which is dc power supply and rechargable. Alternative power solutions like DC microgrids or standalone renewable energy systems may offer more favorable options for specific agricultural needs
